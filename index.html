About the App

AS-Player is a powerful, native Android video player built for performance and simplicity. It supports a wide range of video and audio formats through advanced FFmpeg integration.

Key Features

✓ High-Performance FFmpeg Decoding
✓ Real-time Subtitle Resyncing
✓ 5-Band Equalizer & Bass Boost
✓ Smart Media Library Management
