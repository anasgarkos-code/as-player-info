Privacy Policy for AS-Player

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information Collection

AS-Player is a local media player. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. All file processing (Video/Audio) happens locally on your device.

We require access to your device's storage (Media Library) to scan and play your local video and audio files. We also use the Internet permission solely for loading advertisements via Google AdMob.

We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect and use anonymous data (such as your Advertising ID) to show relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more details.
